What is Autism?

Autism spectrum disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition that affects how people communicate, interact, and experience the world. Every person with autism is unique, with their own strengths and challenges.

Signs & Early Intervention

Early signs may include difficulties with social interaction, communication, and repetitive behaviors. Early intervention can greatly improve outcomes for children with autism.

Strengths & Abilities

Many people with autism have exceptional abilities in areas such as memory, music, math, or art. Recognizing and supporting these strengths is important.

Support & Acceptance

Acceptance, understanding, and support from family, friends, and the community can make a big difference in the lives of people with autism.
